# Platform Model — Clinique MANA

Clinique MANA is a client-to-professional routing and billing platform.

Responsibilities:
- Collect client intake information (motifs, preferences, consent)
- Maintain professional profiles and availability
- Recommend and assign clients to professionals
- Bill clients directly (QuickBooks)
- Track revenue at the platform level
- Calculate professional compensation
- Execute monthly payouts to professionals

Non-responsibilities (current phases):
- Running or scheduling appointments internally
- Managing session calendars
- Storing session recordings or clinical documentation
- Acting as the healthcare provider

Appointments:
- Are currently handled via GoRendezvous
- Are primarily virtual (Zoom)
- MAY be internalized in later phases (explicit future scope)

Financial model:
- Professionals define their hourly rate
- Clinique MANA bills the client
- Clinique MANA retains a 25–30% commission
- Professionals are compensated monthly

Key principle:
Clinique MANA owns the client relationship and billing.
Professionals own the clinical relationship and session execution.
